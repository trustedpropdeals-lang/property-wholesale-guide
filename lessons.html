Real Estate Investing Lesson Plan

A multi‑chapter guide to mastering property wholesaling and rental arbitrage

Chapter 1: Foundations of Property Wholesaling

Begin with the basics: what wholesaling is, how it differs from traditional flipping, and why it’s attractive to new investors.

  • Definition of property wholesaling
  • Understanding assignment fees and profit spreads
  • Legal considerations and licensing requirements

Chapter 2: Finding Motivated Sellers & Properties

Learn how to find distressed or undervalued properties and connect with motivated sellers.

  • Driving for dollars and direct mail campaigns
  • Using MLS and foreclosure auctions to source deals
  • Pre‑screening leads and estimating repair costs

Chapter 3: Building Your Buyers Network

A strong buyers list ensures your deals move quickly. Explore strategies for growing and nurturing this network.

  • Finding cash buyers through REIAs, networking events and online platforms
  • Segmenting your buyers based on criteria (location, budget, asset class)
  • Maintaining relationships and communicating deals effectively

Chapter 4: Negotiating & Assigning Contracts

Master the negotiation process and learn how to secure and assign contracts legally and ethically.

  • Key elements of a purchase contract and assignment clause
  • Calculating maximum allowable offers (MAO)
  • Closing through a title company or attorney

Chapter 5: Fundamentals of Rental Arbitrage

Discover how rental arbitrage works and why it can be a powerful strategy when buying is out of reach.

  • Overview of rental arbitrage and how it differs from wholesaling
  • Understanding lease agreements and landlord negotiations
  • Calculating profitability with occupancy rates and nightly rates

Chapter 6: Market Analysis & Property Selection

Learn how to identify high‑demand markets and select properties that maximize rental income.

  • Using tools like AirDNA to analyze short‑term rental markets
  • Selecting properties near tourist attractions, business districts and hospitals
  • Budgeting for furnishings, utilities and operating costs

Chapter 7: Launching & Managing Your Rental Arbitrage Business

Ready to go live? Launch your rental with a professional listing and manage guest experiences efficiently.

  • Creating standout listings with professional photos and descriptions
  • Dynamic pricing and revenue management tools
  • Automation and systems for guest communication and cleaning

Chapter 8: Tools, Resources & Next Steps

Equip yourself with the software and communities that can accelerate your investing journey.

  • Deal-analysis calculators and CRM platforms for wholesalers (e.g., REsimpli)
  • Market data and revenue forecasting platforms for rental arbitrage (e.g., AirDNA)
  • Educational resources: RealEstateSkills, FortuneBuilders, BiggerPockets and local REIAs
