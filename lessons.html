Real Estate Investing Lesson Plan

Learn the essentials of property wholesaling and rental arbitrage through step-by-step chapters.

Chapter 1: Foundations of Property Wholesaling

  • Understand what wholesaling is and how it differs from fix-and-flip.
  • Learn about assignment fees, profit spreads, and why wholesalers don’t take ownership.
  • Review legal considerations and licensing requirements in your state.
Start Interactive Lesson

Chapter 2: Finding Motivated Sellers & Properties

  • Drive for dollars, search MLS and foreclosure listings, and network with realtors.
  • Use skip tracing, cold calling and direct mail campaigns to reach distressed owners.
  • Screen leads and run comparative market analyses (CMA) to gauge deal potential.

Chapter 3: Building a Buyers Network

  • Create and nurture a list of cash buyers and investor contacts.
  • Attend local REIA meetings, auctions and networking events.
  • Leverage social media, Craigslist and real estate forums to find buyers.

Chapter 4: Negotiating & Assigning Contracts

  • Craft purchase agreements with assignability clauses.
  • Negotiate purchase price and terms with sellers.
  • Assign the contract to a buyer and manage closing with a title company.

Chapter 5: Rental Arbitrage Fundamentals

  • Understand the concept of leasing properties and subletting as short-term rentals.
  • Compare long-term rent, occupancy rates and daily rates to ensure profitability.
  • Learn how rental arbitrage fits into overall real estate investing strategy.

Chapter 6: Market Analysis & Property Selection

  • Research STR regulations, taxes and neighborhood zoning requirements.
  • Use tools like AirDNA to analyze occupancy rates and ADRs in target markets.
  • Select properties near tourist attractions, business districts and amenities.

Chapter 7: Launching & Managing Rental Arbitrage

  • Negotiate leases with landlords that permit STR subletting.
  • Furnish the property, create attractive listings with professional photos.
  • Manage guest communication, cleaning and pricing to maximize occupancy.

Chapter 8: Tools & Resources for Success

  • Deal analysis software (e.g., REsimpli, Propstream) for wholesaling.
  • Short-term rental data tools like AirDNA for rental arbitrage.
  • Educational communities such as BiggerPockets, REIAs, and training courses.
