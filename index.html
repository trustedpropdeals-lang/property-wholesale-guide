What is Property Wholesaling?

Property wholesaling is a strategy where investors find properties below market value, sign a purchase agreement, and then assign that contract to another buyer for a fee. Unlike flipping, wholesalers don't renovate or hold the property; they simply secure the right to purchase and pass it along.

  • Low capital requirements: you don't need to close on the property yourself
  • Focus on finding motivated sellers and distressed properties
  • Earn assignment fees by connecting sellers with cash buyers

Steps to Start Wholesaling

  1. Market Research: Understand local real estate laws and market trends to identify opportunities.
  2. Build a Buyersâ€™ List: Network with cash buyers and investors who are ready to purchase deals quickly.
  3. Find Distressed Properties: Use MLS, driving for dollars, direct mail, and online tools to locate motivated sellers.
  4. Negotiate and Secure Contracts: Get properties under contract at favorable prices with assignable clauses.
  5. Assign the Contract: Transfer the purchase agreement to a buyer for a fee and close through a title company or attorney.

What is Rental Arbitrage?

Rental arbitrage involves leasing a property long-term and then re-renting it short-term on platforms like Airbnb. By capturing the spread between long-term rent and higher short-term rates, you can generate cash flow without owning the asset.

  • Skip large down payments and mortgages by leasing instead of buying
  • Operate in high-demand travel markets to maximize occupancy
  • Ensure leases permit subletting and compliance with local regulations

Steps to Start Rental Arbitrage

  1. Understand Regulations: Research local short-term rental laws and HOA rules.
  2. Choose the Right Property: Select homes in popular neighborhoods with attractive amenities.
  3. Negotiate Lease Terms: Secure landlord approval for subletting and set clear expectations.
  4. Calculate Profit Margins: Account for rent, furnishings, utilities, cleaning, fees, and taxes.
  5. Launch Professionally: Use high-quality photos, dynamic pricing tools, and excellent guest service.
