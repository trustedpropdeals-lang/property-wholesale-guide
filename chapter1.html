Chapter 1: Foundations of Property Wholesaling

Learn what property wholesaling is and why it's a powerful entry strategy.

What You'll Learn

Property wholesaling involves finding undervalued real estate, securing the rights to purchase via a contract, and assigning that contract to another buyer for a fee. In this lesson, you'll explore the fundamentals of wholesaling, including how wholesalers profit, legal considerations, and why wholesaling requires little capital.

Explore the Wholesaling Process

Click each step below to reveal more details.

  • 1. Find Motivated Sellers
    • Drive for dollars, search MLS and foreclosure listings, network with realtors, and use direct mail and cold calling to find distressed properties.
  • 2. Build a Buyers List
    • Create and nurture a list of cash buyers through REI meetings, social media and networking events to ensure you have interested investors ready.
  • 3. Negotiate & Secure Contract
    • Negotiate a purchase agreement that allows assignment, ensuring the contract price leaves room for an assignment fee.
  • 4. Assign & Close
    • Assign the contract to your buyer, collect your assignment fee at closing, and celebrate your successful wholesale deal!
