Property Wholesaling & Arbitrage Guide

Learn how to profit from real estate without owning property through wholesaling contracts and rental arbitrage.

What is Property Wholesaling?

Property wholesaling is a real estate strategy where investors secure a property under contract at a below‑market price and then assign that contract to a buyer for a fee. The wholesaler never takes possession of the property; instead they profit by connecting motivated sellers with cash buyers.

  • Low capital requirement: no down payments or mortgages.
  • Quick turnaround: assignments can close in days or weeks.
  • Focus on finding distressed or undervalued properties.

Steps to Start Wholesaling

  1. Market research: Understand local laws, property values and buyer demand.
  2. Build a buyers list: Network with cash buyers and investors.
  3. Find distressed properties: Look for off‑market deals, foreclosures and motivated sellers.
  4. Negotiate and secure contract: Get a purchase agreement with an assignment clause.
  5. Assign the contract: Transfer the contract to a buyer for a fee; use a title company or attorney to close.

What is Rental Arbitrage?

Rental arbitrage involves leasing a property long‑term and then re‑renting it on short‑term platforms like Airbnb to capture higher nightly rates. Investors take advantage of strong tourism demand and avoid mortgage debt, but must comply with local short‑term rental regulations.

  • Accessible entry: bypasses the need for large down payments.
  • High yield: nightly income can exceed monthly rent costs.
  • Flexibility: operate in multiple markets using leases.

Steps to Start Rental Arbitrage

  1. Research laws: Check local short‑term rental regulations and HOA rules.
  2. Select a profitable market: Use tools like AirDNA to evaluate occupancy rates and average daily rates.
  3. Negotiate lease terms: Get landlord permission to sublet and operate STRs.
  4. Calculate margins: Budget for rent, furnishings, utilities, cleaning and platform fees.
  5. Launch professionally: Use professional photos, dynamic pricing, and provide excellent guest experiences.
